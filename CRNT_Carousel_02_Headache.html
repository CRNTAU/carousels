Most people reach for paracetamol. Or another coffee.
Neither fixes a sodium deficit. And that is usually what is behind a headache that keeps coming back.
03 / 07
Here is the mechanism.
Sodium governs fluid distribution in your body. When sodium drops, blood volume drops. Less perfusion to the brain. That is the headache.
04 / 07
Drinking more water can make it worse.
Without sodium, extra fluid dilutes what is already low. You hydrate. The headache stays.
